RESEARCH ETHICS & INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Research Ethics:
- Institutional Review Board (IRB): Approval required for human subject research
- Animal Ethics: Committee for animal research compliance
- Environmental Impact: Assessment for environmental research
- Data Privacy: GDPR and data protection compliance
- Publication Ethics: Plagiarism and misconduct prevention

Intellectual Property (IP):
- Patent Filing: 20+ patents filed annually
- IP Committee: Oversees patent management
- Patent Grants: Ownership shared with inventors (College 50%, Inventor 50%)
- Licensing: Commercial licensing of college IP
- Trademark Protection: College branding protection

IP Management Process:
1. Invention Disclosure: Researcher submits innovation details
2. Evaluation: IP committee assesses patentability and commercial potential
3. Patent Filing: Legal assistance in Indian and international patent filing
4. Commercialization: Support in technology transfer to industry
5. Revenue Sharing: Royalties distributed to inventors and college

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How do I publish my research?
A: Contact Research Office with paper draft. We provide guidance on journal selection, submission, and publication ethics.

Q: Can I patent my research?
A: Yes, submit invention disclosure to IP Committee. They evaluate patentability and assist in filing.

Q: How do I get research funding?
A: Research Office helps in identifying suitable funding agencies and writing grant proposals.

Q: Can I collaborate with industry on research?
A: Yes, 100+ industry partnerships available. Contact Industry Collaboration office.

Q: What international opportunities are available?
A: Student exchange, joint research, dual degrees, and international funding through partnerships.

Q: How much funding is available for startup ideas?
A: Seed grants up to Rs 25 Lakhs for validated ideas. Rs 100 Lakhs for scaling ventures.
