FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: What is the typical placement timeline?
A: Placements typically begin in September and complete by December. Some offers come in January-February for off-campus rounds.

Q: Is placement mandatory?
A: No, placement is optional. Students can pursue higher studies or entrepreneurship instead.

Q: What is the guarantee on placement?
A: We cannot guarantee placement, but with 94% placement rate and 375+ companies visiting, opportunities are excellent. Success depends on student effort and performance.

Q: Can I sit for multiple companies?
A: Yes, you can appear for multiple companies and choose the best offer (within college guidelines).

Q: What happens if I don't get placed?
A: We provide extended support through: off-campus placements, entrepreneurship guidance, higher studies support, contract jobs with conversion possibility.

Q: Do startups provide internship/placement opportunities?
A: Yes, 50+ startups recruit from our college with competitive salaries and equity options.

Q: What support is given for higher studies abroad?
A: Coaching, application guidance, GMAT/GRE preparation, visa support, and scholarship information.

Q: Are there any campus restrictions on company selection?
A: No, students can apply to any company. College only ensures all eligible candidates get equal opportunity.
