INSTITUTION OVERVIEW
EduReach College is a premier autonomous engineering and technology institution established in 2005. We are located in the heart of Hyderabad, Telangana, India, spread across a sprawling 35-acre green campus.

Accreditation & Affiliations:
- AICTE Approved (All India Council for Technical Education)
- Autonomous Institution by UGC
- ISO 9001:2015 Certified
