MERIT-BASED SCHOLARSHIPS

Academic Excellence Scholarship:
- Criteria: CGPA ≥ 8.5 in previous semester
- Benefit: 50% tuition fee waiver
- Renewal: Annually based on CGPA maintenance
- Number of Awards: 200+ annually
- Process: Automatically awarded, no application needed

Merit Scholarship - Top Performers:
- Criteria: Top 10% of class (CGPA ≥ 9.0)
- Benefit: 75% tuition fee waiver
- Additional Benefit: Laptop allowance Rs 30,000 one-time
- Renewal: Till graduation if CGPA maintained
- Number of Awards: 50+ annually

Research Scholar Scholarship:
- Criteria: Published research paper(s) in peer-reviewed journals
- Benefit: Rs 10,000 per published paper + 25% fee waiver
- Duration: Until graduation
- Maximum Awards: Unlimited
- Process: Application through department

Innovation Scholarship:
- Criteria: Invention, patent filed, or innovative project
- Benefit: Rs 25,000-1,00,000 + 50% fee waiver (2 years)
- Process: Application through Innovation Center
- Awards: 20+ annually

SCHOLARSHIP APPLICATION PROCESS

Step 1: Check Eligibility
- Visit college website: www.edureach.edu.in/scholarships
- Read eligibility criteria for each scheme

Step 2: Submit Application
- Online portal: scholarships.edureach.edu.in
- OR Paper application to Financial Aid Office
- Deadline: Usually 2 weeks into semester

Step 3: Document Submission
- Income Certificate (for need-based)
- Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS)
- Entrance Score Card
- CGPA Certificate from previous institution
- Bank Passbook copy
- Aadhaar Card

Step 4: Verification
- Financial Aid team verifies documents (5-7 days)
- Personal interview if needed

Step 5: Approval & Disbursal
- Approval announcement: Within 15 days
- Fee waiver processed next semester
- Cash awards credited to bank account within 7 days

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

Maintenance of Scholarship:
- Most scholarships require minimum CGPA maintenance (varies by scheme)
- Failure to maintain may result in scholarship withdrawal
- Can reapply if CGPA improves

Multiple Scholarships:
- Students can apply for multiple scholarships
- Maximum benefit capped at 100% fee waiver
- One financial aid benefit per student

Scholarship Duration:
- Undergraduate: 4 years (B.Tech)
- Postgraduate: 2 years (M.Tech/MBA)
- Renewal: Annual based on criteria maintenance

Loan & Scholarship Combination:
- Can combine scholarship with education loan (loan covers remaining fees)
- EMI becomes lower with scholarship benefit

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Can I apply for scholarship after admission?
A: Yes, most scholarships accept applications after enrollment. Check specific deadlines.

Q: If I get multiple scholarships, do I get all benefits?
A: Maximum combined benefit is 100% fee waiver to avoid over-subsidization.

Q: What if my CGPA drops below maintenance threshold?
A: Your scholarship may be suspended. You can reapply if CGPA improves next semester.

Q: Is scholarship adjusted if I get education loan?
A: No, scholarship and loan are separate. You pay remaining amount after scholarship from loan.

Q: Can scholarship be combined with part-time job earnings?
A: Yes, earning from part-time job doesn't affect scholarship eligibility.

Q: What happens to scholarship during academic break/holidays?
A: Scholarship continues across all semesters if criteria are maintained.

Q: Can I defer scholarship to next year?
A: No, scholarship benefits for that year are forfeited if not claimed.
