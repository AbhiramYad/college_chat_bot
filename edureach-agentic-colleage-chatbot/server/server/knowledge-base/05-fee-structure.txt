PAYMENT OPTIONS & PLANS

Payment Schedule:
- Semester 1: 50% of annual fee + Registration fee
- Semester 2: 50% of annual fee
- Total paid in 2 installments per academic year

Payment Methods:
- Online Payment: Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking
- Check/DD: Payable to "EduReach College"
- Bank Transfer: Account details provided at admission
- EMI Options: Through partner banks (SBI, HDFC, ICICI)

---

EDUCATION LOAN FACILITIES

Partner Banks:
1. State Bank of India (SBI)
   - Loan Amount: Up to Rs 20 Lakhs
   - Interest Rate: 8.5% - 9.5% per annum
   - Margin: 0% (No margin for loans up to Rs 5 Lakhs)
   - Repayment: 10-15 years after course completion

2. HDFC Bank
   - Loan Amount: Up to Rs 20 Lakhs
   - Interest Rate: 8.75% - 10% per annum
   - Flexible EMI starting post-course

3. ICICI Bank
   - Loan Amount: Up to Rs 20 Lakhs
   - Interest Rate: 8.5% - 10.5% per annum
   - Processing Fee: 1% of loan amount

Eligibility:
- Valid admission offer or enrolled student
- Indian citizen
- Age: 18-40 years for education loan
- Minimum annual family income: No minimum

REFUND POLICY

Cancellation Within 7 Days: 100% refund of fees (except registration fee)
Cancellation After 7 Days: 80% refund of fees (except registration fee)
Cancellation After 15 Days: 60% refund of fees (except registration fee)
Cancellation After 1 Month: 40% refund of fees (except registration fee)
After 1st Semester: No refund, but withdrawal allowed with "Credited/Withdrawn" status
