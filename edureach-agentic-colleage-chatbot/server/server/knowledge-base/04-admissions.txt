UNDERGRADUATE ADMISSIONS (B.Tech)

Eligibility Requirements:
- 10+2 (PCM) with minimum 60% aggregate marks
- Physics and Chemistry minimum 50% each
- Mathematics minimum 50%
- Valid entrance exam score

Admission Routes:

ROUTE 1: Merit/Entrance Exam Based (60% Seats)
- EAMCET (TS EAMCET or AP EAMCET) Score based
- Rank in Top 5000 in state/national level
- Merit Cut-off (2024): AIR 1-5000 (varies by branch)
- Counseling conducted by state government
- Online choice filling and seat allocation

ROUTE 2: Management Quota (30% Seats)
- Direct admission without entrance exam
- 12th board marks considered for ranking
- Personal interview (15-20 minutes)
- Merit consideration: 12th aggregate + interview performance
- Application submission: June-July (ongoing basis till July 15)

ROUTE 3: Lateral Entry (Diploma/B.Sc) (10% Seats)
- Diploma holders from polytechnics
- B.Sc graduates with Physics & Chemistry
- Direct entry into 2nd year B.Tech
- Merit-based selection on entrance exam

IMPORTANT DATES (2024-2025 Academic Year):
- Application Portal Opening: March 1, 2024
- Application Deadline (Entrance Route): April 30, 2024
- Application Deadline (Management Quota): July 15, 2024
- Entrance Exam Date: June 15, 2024
- Merit List Release: July 20, 2024
- Counseling Start: July 25, 2024
- Seat Allocation: August 1, 2024
- Classes Begin: August 10, 2024

Documents Required:
- 10th Mark Sheet (Original + 2 copies)
- 12th Mark Sheet (Original + 2 copies)
- Entrance Score Card (Original + 2 copies)
- Transfer Certificate from previous school
- Migration Certificate (if applicable)
- Caste Certificate (if applicable for reserved category)
- Birth Certificate or Aadhaar Card
- 6 Passport Size Photographs (4x6 cm)
- Valid ID Proof (Aadhaar/Passport/Voter ID)
- Category Certificate (General/SC/ST/OBC/EWS)
- Income Certificate (if applicable for scholarship)

Fees for Management Quota Candidates:
- Application Fee: Rs 500 (Non-refundable)
- Registration Fee: Rs 3,000 (At the time of admission)
- Refundable Caution Money: Rs 5,000 (Returned on graduation)

POSTGRADUATE ADMISSIONS (M.Tech/MBA)

M.TECH ADMISSIONS:
Eligibility:
- Bachelor's degree in engineering/technology or equivalent
- Minimum 60% aggregate marks (55% for SC/ST)
- Valid GATE Score OR College Entrance Exam

Admission Routes:
- GATE Merit (50% Seats): GATE Rank in Top 300
- College Entrance (50% Seats): Entrance exam conducted by college (60 questions, 90 minutes)

Important Dates:
- Application Opening: March 15, 2024
- Application Deadline: May 31, 2024
- Gate Score Submission: June 15, 2024
- Entrance Exam Date: June 22, 2024
- Merit List Release: July 5, 2024
- Admission Process: July 10-15, 2024
- Classes Begin: July 25, 2024

---

MBA ADMISSIONS:
Eligibility:
- Bachelor's degree in any stream with minimum 50% aggregate marks
- Valid CAT Score (Top 50 percentile preferred) OR MAT Score OR ATMA Score

Selection Process:
- Score: 80% weightage
- Academic Profile & Work Experience: 10% weightage
- Interview & Group Discussion: 10% weightage

Important Dates:
- Application Opening: April 1, 2024
- Application Deadline: June 30, 2024
- Entrance Exam (CAT/MAT): Conducted by respective bodies
- Shortlist for Interview: July 20, 2024
- Final Merit List: August 10, 2024
- Admission Process: August 15-25, 2024
- Classes Begin: August 30, 2024

Specialization Selection: At time of admission or after 1st semester based on performance

PH.D. ADMISSIONS:

Eligibility:
- M.Tech degree or equivalent research qualification
- OR B.Tech with valid GATE/NET/CSIR-NET score
- Minimum 60% marks in qualifying degree (55% for SC/ST)

Selection Process:
- Written Entrance Test: 50 marks
- Research Proposal Presentation: 50 marks
- Interview with Faculty: 50 marks
- Total Score: 150 marks

Application Timeline:
- Application Opening: January & July (Two intakes per year)
- Application Deadline: March 15 & September 15
- Entrance Exam: April 1 & October 1
- Interview: April 15-20 & October 15-20
- Result Announcement: April 30 & October 31
- Registration: May 15 & November 15

SCHOLARSHIP & FINANCIAL AID

Merit Scholarship:
- Top 10% CGPA: 50% tuition fee waiver
- 10-20% CGPA: 30% tuition fee waiver
- Automatically awarded, no application needed
- Renewed annually based on performance

Need-Based Scholarship:
- For students with family income < Rs 5 LPA: Up to 100% fee waiver
- Family income Rs 5-10 LPA: 50% fee waiver
- Application required with income certificate
- 100+ scholarships awarded annually

SC/ST/OBC Scholarship:
- Government reimbursement of tuition fees
- Reserved category students eligible
- State-sponsored assistance
- Hostel fee concession (50% off)

Sports Scholarship:

- Selection based on credentials
- 20+ scholarships available annually

Girls Education Encouragement:
- 30% fee waiver for girl students
- Additional 20% for economically weaker girls
- Priority hostel allocation

First-Generation Learners:
- 40% tuition fee waiver
- Mentorship program included
- Career counseling assistance

FREQUENTLY ASKED ADMISSION QUESTIONS:

Q: Can I apply for multiple branches?
A: Yes, you can apply for all branches in order of preference. Final allocation based on merit and availability.

Q: Is work experience required for MBA admission?
A: No, but it's preferred. Recent graduates are also eligible.

Q: When will I get admission letter?
A: Within 5 days of completing admission process after seat allocation.

Q: Can I defer admission to next year?
A: Yes, deferment request must be submitted within 15 days of seat allocation. Only 1-year deferment allowed.

Q: Are there provisions for PWD (Persons with Disabilities)?
A: Yes, 5% seats reserved. Special arrangements for accessibility provided.

Q: Can NRI/Foreign students apply?
A: Yes, separate quota for 20 NRI seats. Management quota available. Fees vary, contact admissions.

Q: What is the refund policy for application fee?
A: Application fee is non-refundable. Acceptance fee refundable if seat is not taken.
