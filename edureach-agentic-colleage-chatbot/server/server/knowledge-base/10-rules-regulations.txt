============================================
RULES, REGULATIONS & STUDENT POLICIES
============================================

ACADEMIC REGULATIONS

Attendance Policy:
- Minimum Attendance: 75% of classes per subject to be eligible for semester exams
- Medical Leave: Covered by provision (up to 10 days per semester with medical certificate)
- Attendance Tracking: Real-time via RFID card system and biometric
- Low Attendance: Notification sent to student and parents
- Exemption: Extraordinary cases considered by Dean Academics upon application

Assessment & Evaluation:
- Continuous Evaluation: Assignments, quizzes, projects (40% weightage)
- Mid-Semester Exam: Conducted in 7th-8th week (20% weightage)
- End-Semester Exam: Final comprehensive exam (40% weightage)
- Grading Scale: A+, A, B+, B, C+, C, D, F (Point scale 10-0)
- CGPA Calculation: Cumulative Grade Point Average across all semesters

Academic Honesty & Plagiarism:
- Definition: Plagiarism includes direct copying, paraphrasing without credit, data fabrication
- Consequences: First offense - F in course, Second offense - Suspension, Third - Expulsion
- Plagiarism Check: All submissions checked using Turnitin software
- Allowed Plagiarism: Up to 15% similarity index acceptable
- Citation Standards: APA/IEEE/MLA as per department requirement

Course Registration:
- Maximum Courses: Per semester as per academic plan (usually 24 credits)
- Minimum CGPA: 2.0 required to register for next semester
- Course Prerequisites: Strictly enforced, prior permission needed for exceptions
- Add/Drop Period: First 2 weeks of semester
- Audit Courses: Can audit up to 2 courses per semester (free, no grade recorded)

Academic Performance Standards:
- Minimum GPA: 2.0 to continue enrollment
- Probation: CGPA below 2.0 → put on academic probation
- Warning: Probation for 2 consecutive semesters → show cause letter
- Dismissal: Failure to improve CGPA → academic dismissal from college

Transcripts & Certificates:
- Semester Transcripts: Available 2 weeks after results declared
- Final Degree Certificate: After graduation ceremony completion
- Duplicate Certificates: Available on request with nominal fee (Rs 500)
- Verification: Online verification system for degree authenticity

HOSTEL REGULATIONS

Hostel Check-In & Check-Out:
- Check-In: Within 3 days of admission
- Check-Out: Within 7 days after final exams/graduation
- Late Check-Out: Additional charges apply
- Summer Vacation: Hostel closed during vacation, baggage storage available
- Emergency Checkout: Prior notice required to hostel warden

Room Allocation:
- Single Rooms: Available for special needs/physical disability
- Double/Triple Rooms: Standard allocation
- Room Changes: After 1st semester only with valid reason and approval
- Room Inspection: Monthly checks for cleanliness and safety
- Hostel Damage: Liability on occupants, cost recovery from caution money

Hostel Rules & Code of Conduct:
- Curfew: 10 PM weekdays, 12 AM weekends (lockdown at curfew time)
- Overnight Pass: Needed for staying outside hostel, available from warden
- Visitor Policy: Guests allowed only in common areas during specified hours
- Guest Registration: All guests must register at hostel office
- Room Parties: Banned, common areas available for celebrations
- Prohibited Items: Weapons, explosives, alcohol, drugs, cooking equipment

Safety & Security:
- Key Responsibility: Student liable for loss or damage to room keys
- Lost Keys: Replacement charges: Rs 500 per key
- Room Security: Use provided locks, secure valuables
- CCTV Coverage: 24/7 monitoring of common areas, entry/exit
- Emergency Bell: Available in every room for assistance

Hostel Facilities & Maintenance:
- Housekeeping: Daily cleaning of common areas, weekly room cleaning
- Maintenance: Repair requests submitted via online portal (response within 24 hrs)
- Electrical Issues: Report to hostel electrician immediately
- Water Issues: Contact hostel maintenance office
- Internet: Wi-Fi 24/7 available at no extra cost

Hostel Discipline:
- Ragging: Completely banned, severe action against perpetrators
- Noise Policy: Quiet time 10 PM - 7 AM, violations result in fines
- Smoking/Drinking: Strictly prohibited, expulsion risk
- Violence: Zero-tolerance, immediate expulsion
- Theft: Campus police involvement, expulsion for offenders

Hostel Fees & Deposit:
- Room Rent: Rs 60,000-1,20,000 per year (varies by room type)
- Mess Charges: Rs 40,000-50,000 per year
- Utilities: Included in room rent
- Caution Money: Rs 10,000 (refundable at checkout, deducted for damages)
- Late Payment: 2% surcharge per month on outstanding fees

DISCIPLINE & GRIEVANCE PROCEDURES

Student Code of Conduct:
- Respect: Towards faculty, staff, and fellow students
- Honesty: Academic integrity, truthful representation
- Responsibility: Adherence to college regulations and policies
- Safety: Respect for personal safety and others' safety
- Citizenship: Compliance with laws and college policies

Dress Code:
- Monday-Friday: Business formal (blazer/shirt-pants or salwar suit)
- Saturday: College branded t-shirt with college logo
- Special Events: As specified for event
- Casual Dress: Permitted in hostel and weekends outside campus
- ID Card: Mandatory at all times on campus

Prohibited Activities:
- Substance Abuse: Smoking, alcohol, drugs - expulsion risk
- Violence: Any form of physical aggression - immediate expulsion
- Harassment: Sexual, verbal, or cyber harassment - disciplinary action
- Cheating: Academic dishonesty - zero tolerance
- Unauthorized Absence: Skipping classes without leave

Disciplinary Procedures:
- Verbal Warning: For minor violations
- Written Warning: Recorded in file for repeated minor violations
- Suspension: 1-7 days for serious violations
- Expulsion: For heinous crimes (ragging, violence, drugs)
- Appeal Process: Student can appeal within 15 days of action

Grievance Redressal:
- Ombudsman Office: Neutral complaint resolution for student grievances
- Student Court: Peer-based disciplinary action review
- Appeal to Dean: Final appeal authority
- Timeline: Resolution within 30 days of complaint
- Confidentiality: All complaints handled with confidentiality

LEAVE & ABSENCE POLICIES

Medical Leave:
- Requirement: Medical certificate from registered medical practitioner
- Duration: Up to 10 days per semester
- Process: Notify class advisor and hostel warden within 24 hours
- Documentation: Submit original medical certificate within 3 days

Casual Leave:
- Availability: 5 days per semester (non-cumulative)
- Process: Apply to class advisor with reason
- Notification: Inform hostel warden if on hostel leave
- Approval: Class advisor approval required
- Attendance Impact: Counted as absent on leave dates

Duty Leave:
- Eligibility: For sports, competitions, club activities (approved)
- Process: Faculty advisor/principal must approve duty
- Documentation: Duty letter required from organizer
- Attendance: Covered (full attendance credited)
- Condition: Only for pre-approved official duties

Extraordinary Leave:
- For emergencies: Death in family, serious illness
- Duration: Case-by-case approval by Dean Academics
- Documentation: Medical certificate or death certificate required
- Limited: Exceptional cases only

Fine & Penalties:
- Unauthorized Absence: Rs 500 per day
- Late Arrival: Rs 100 per occurrence
- Curfew Violation: Rs 1000-5000 depending on severity
- Missing Assignment: 10% marks deduction

Exam Registration:
- Deadline: 2 weeks before exam commencement
- Process: Online portal registration
- Late Registration: Possible with late fee (Rs 1000)
- Eligibility: Minimum 75% attendance required

Exam Hall Rules:
- Entry: Arrive 10 minutes before exam start
- ID Card: Mandatory, without ID - not allowed to sit
- Prohibited Items: Mobile phone, calculator, notes (unless specified)
- Seating: Random seat allocation
- Exam Duration: Standard exam duration, no extra time
- Question Clarification: Invigilator clarifies only language-related doubts

Unfair Means & Cheating:
- Detection: Immediate expulsion from exam hall
- Consequence: F grade in that course
- Reporting: Case filed with disciplinary committee
- Penalty: Varies from suspension to expulsion
- Zero Tolerance: No leniency for cheating

Answer Script Submission:
- At End: All rough work, answer scripts must be submitted
- Incomplete Answers: Will be evaluated as submitted
- Penalty for Late Submission: None, but writing beyond time may not be evaluated
- Answer Scripts: Retained for 2 months after result declaration

Re-evaluation & Rechecking:
- Fee: Rs 500 per subject
- Timeline: Within 15 days of result declaration
- Process: Apply through department office
- Evaluation: Checked by different faculty member
- Result Change: If justified, result updated

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: What is the minimum CGPA to continue?
A: 2.0 (D grade equivalent). Below this requires academic probation/dismissal.

Q: Can I change my hostel room after 1st semester?
A: Yes, with valid reason and hostel office approval.

Q: What happens if I fail a course?
A: You must retake the course next semester. Highest grade between attempts recorded.

Q: Is ragging allowed in any form?
A: Absolutely not. Even "friendly" ragging results in immediate disciplinary action.

Q: Can I appeal against my grade?
A: Yes, submit appeal within 15 days of result declaration with reasons.

Q: What if I have a medical emergency during exam?
A: Report to invigilator immediately. Medical certificate needed for make-up exam.
