FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: What are the office hours?
A: Generally 9 AM - 5 PM (Mon-Fri). Some offices operate Saturdays. See specific timings above.

Q: Can I meet officials without appointment?
A: Most departments accept walk-ins during office hours, but appointment recommended for senior officials.

Q: How do I file a grievance?
A: Use online grievance portal or visit Ombudsman office. Forms available at Dean of Students office.

Q: What is the process for certificate/transcript request?
A: Visit Registrar office with application and payment. Issued within 2 weeks.

Q: Is there a hotline for complaints?
A: Yes, call Dean of Students office or Ombudsman office during office hours or emergency line after hours.

Q: How do I reset my student portal password?
A: Visit IT Help Desk or use "Forgot Password" on portal. Reset link sent to registered email.

Q: Can I contact college for feedback/suggestions?
A: Yes, submit through student portal feedback form or email directly to dean.students@edureach.edu.in
