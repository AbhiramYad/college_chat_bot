FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Can I join multiple clubs?
A: Yes, students can join up to 5 clubs based on interest and time management.

Q: Do I need to participate in events?
A: Participation is optional, but highly encouraged for holistic development.

Q: What is the hostel check-in process?
A: Report to hostel office with documents, room inspection, key handover, registration.

Q: Can parents visit hostel?
A: Yes, supervised visits during designated hours (Saturdays 2-6 PM).

Q: Is there a dress code for attending events?
A: Generally no, casual attire acceptable. Specific dress codes mentioned in event details.

Q: How do I report security issues on campus?
A: Contact security office directly or use emergency hotline +91-40-23894999.

Q: Are pets allowed in hostels?
A: No, strict no-pet policy for hygiene and safety reasons.

Q: Can I participate in events outside college?
A: Yes, with prior permission from faculty advisor and student affairs office.
